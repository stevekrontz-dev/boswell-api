# Cancellation Policy

*Last updated: January 15, 2026*

## How to Cancel

You can cancel your Boswell subscription at any time:

1. **Dashboard:** Go to Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription
2. **Email:** Contact billing@askboswell.com

Cancellation takes effect immediately. You won't be charged again.

## What Happens to Your Data

### Immediately

- Access to paid features ends
- Your memories remain accessible in read-only mode

### After 30 Days

- Your memories are permanently deleted from active systems
- Your account is deactivated

### After 60 Days

- Your data is permanently deleted from all backups
- No recovery is possible

## Export Your Data First

Before canceling, you can export your memories:

1. Go to Settings → Data → Export
2. Download your memories as JSON
3. Cancel when ready

We cannot recover data after deletion. Export first if you want to keep anything.

## Reactivating

Changed your mind?

- **Within 30 days:** Contact us and we can restore your account with all data intact
- **After 30 days:** You'll need to create a new account and start fresh

## Refunds

We don't automatically prorate unused subscription time. If you feel you deserve a refund, contact billing@askboswell.com and we'll work with you. See our Refund Policy for details.
