# Acceptable Use Policy

*Last updated: January 15, 2026*

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") describes what you can and cannot do with Boswell. By using our Services, you agree to comply with this policy.

We want Boswell to be useful for everyone. That means we need some ground rules.

## Use Boswell for Good

Boswell is designed to help you work more effectively with AI by giving it persistent memory. Use it for:

- Maintaining project context across conversations
- Storing decisions, research, and important information
- Coordinating work across multiple AI sessions
- Building a personal or team knowledge base

## Don't Use Boswell for Evil

You may not use Boswell to:

### Store Illegal Content

- Content that violates any applicable law
- Content that infringes on intellectual property rights
- Stolen data or information obtained through unauthorized access
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) - we will report to authorities

### Harm Others

- Harassment, threats, or abuse directed at individuals
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- Hate speech targeting protected groups
- Coordinating violence or illegal activities
- Stalking or surveillance of individuals

### Abuse AI Systems

- Attempting to jailbreak, manipulate, or bypass AI safety measures
- Storing prompts designed to make Claude behave unethically
- Using Boswell to coordinate AI agents for malicious purposes
- Training competing AI systems on data processed through Boswell

### Spam or Fraud

- Generating spam, phishing content, or scam materials
- Impersonating others or misrepresenting your identity
- Creating fake reviews, testimonials, or social proof
- Manipulating markets or spreading financial misinformation

### Violate Anthropic's Terms

Boswell works with Claude. You must also comply with Anthropic's Acceptable Use Policy. If you wouldn't do it directly with Claude, don't use Boswell to do it.

## Technical Restrictions

You may not:

- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to our systems or other users' data
- Reverse engineer, decompile, or attempt to extract source code
- Interfere with or disrupt the Services
- Use automated tools to scrape or harvest data from the Services
- Exceed reasonable usage limits or abuse API access
- Share API keys or allow unauthorized access to your account
- Resell access to the Services without authorization

## Enforcement

We take violations seriously. Depending on the severity:

1. **Warning:** For minor or first-time violations, we may issue a warning and ask you to correct the behavior.

2. **Suspension:** For repeated or serious violations, we may temporarily suspend your account.

3. **Termination:** For severe violations or continued abuse, we will permanently terminate your account without refund.

4. **Legal action:** For illegal activity, we may report to law enforcement and cooperate with investigations.

We reserve the right to remove content that violates this policy without notice.

## Reporting Violations

If you become aware of violations of this policy, please report them to abuse@askboswell.com. We investigate all reports and take appropriate action.

## Changes

We may update this AUP to address new forms of abuse or clarify existing rules. Significant changes will be communicated via email.

## Questions

Questions about what's acceptable? Ask us at support@askboswell.com before doing something you're unsure about.
